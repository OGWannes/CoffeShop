About Us

Discover the founder of Caffe Wannes

Lotfi Chayeb, a resident of Hammam el Ghezez born in 1961, has left an indelible mark on his illustrious career, particularly through the establishment of the highly successful coffee haven known as Caffe Wannes. Named as a heartfelt tribute to his beloved father, Lotfi's commitment and passion shine through in every aspect of his coffee shop. Inaugurated in 1997, this venture started as a humble coffee shop, gradually transforming into the most renowned destination in the city. The success story is attributed not only to the rich aroma of the coffee but also to the welcoming ambiance meticulously curated by the owner. Lotfi's dedication to maintaining a pristine and inviting environment has become synonymous with Caffe Wannes, making it the go-to spot for those seeking not just great coffee but also a memorable and enjoyable experience.
